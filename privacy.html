Advertising

DoomBlock shows ads through Google AdMob. Depending on your region and consent choice, ads may be personalized or non-personalized; AdMob may collect a device advertising identifier and related data to serve and measure ads. You can review Google's handling of this data at policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites. We do not sell your personal data.

Permissions the app requests

DoomBlock is an app blocker, so it asks for some sensitive permissions. Each is used only for the purpose below:

  • Accessibility service — detects which app is currently in the foreground so a block can be enforced. It does not read, store, or transmit the contents of your screen.
  • Usage access — measures time spent in apps to power your limits and statistics.
  • Display over other apps — shows the blocking screen on top of a blocked app.
  • Device admin (optional, only if you turn on "Prevent Uninstall") — stops the app being uninstalled while a block is active. You can turn this off at any time.
  • Notifications & run in background — keeps the blocking service alive and shows its ongoing notification.

Third parties

We share data only with the service providers needed to run the app:

  • Supabase — authentication and cloud database (your account & backup).
  • Google — Google Sign-In, Google Play services, and AdMob (ads).

Data retention & deletion

We keep your account data until you delete your account. You can delete it at any time inside the app (Settings → your account → Delete account), which permanently removes your account and cloud backup and resets the app on your device. You can also request deletion without the app — see our Account Deletion page.

Children

DoomBlock is not directed to children and is intended for users aged 18 and over.
