Delete your account & data

This page explains how to delete your DoomBlock account and the data linked to it. It applies to the DoomBlock app published by Bros Dolz LLC.

Delete from inside the app (fastest)

  1. Open DoomBlock and go to Settings.
  2. Tap your account (the email row) to open the Account page.
  3. Tap Delete account and follow the confirmation steps.
What gets deleted: your account and cloud backup, including your email or login, streak record, saved settings, and block-list setup, are permanently removed from our systems. The app on your device is also reset to a fresh install. This happens right away and cannot be undone.

Request deletion without the app

If you can no longer open the app, email support@brosdolz.com from the address on your account with the subject "Delete my account". We will check that the request belongs to you and delete your account and cloud backup within 30 days.

Data retention

Once deleted, account data is not kept. Anonymous grouped data that cannot identify you, plus standard advertising data held by Google AdMob under its own policy, may still remain outside your account.

See also our Privacy Policy.
